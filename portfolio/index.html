Hi,


I'm Marissa Yelenik, a sophomore journalism and psychology major at the University of Maryland.
I particularly enjoy writing hard news and political based stories after getting my start as news and online editor of Hammond High School's The Bear Press, but I've recently delved into features. I started working at one of the University of Maryland's student publications, Stories Beneath the Shell, at the beginning of spring semester in 2023 where I was able to try new things and grow.
I've also spent time in a photojournalism class where I learned how to take pictures, what to look for and how to edit them. I've since taken pictures for all of my articles done at Stories Beneath the Shell.
I'm excited to continue broadening my horizons and try new things; thank you for taking the time to look through my work.
